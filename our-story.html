How we got here.

A gap in the market

Our founders, coming from industrial B2B sales, kept seeing the same failure: agencies selling scraped contact lists with zero context, leaving sales teams to do the real qualification work themselves.

Ertrag Go is born

We started with a simple rule: no outreach without a live buying trigger. Our first clients were CNC and precision engineering shops testing the Pay-Per-Meeting model.

Adding the SaaS vertical

As more industrial software companies (ERP, MES, QMS) asked for help reaching enterprise manufacturing buyers, we built a second playbook — LinkedIn-first, SaaS-native outreach.

A revenue partnership model

Today we run both Retainer and Pay-Per-Meeting tracks across India, the US, and global markets — always tied to a percentage of what actually closes.
