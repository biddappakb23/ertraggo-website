How We Work

A 7-step delivery engine.
No shortcuts.

Every lead goes through a rigorous research and validation process before it reaches your team.

01

ICP Workshop

Deep-dive session to define your ideal customer profile, trigger events, deal size, and target geographies. No research begins until this is signed off.

02

Intent Signal Scan

We use Apollo, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, and Clay to find companies showing real buying signals right now — capex announcements, hiring surges, plant expansions.

03

Decision-Maker Mapping

Identify the real buyer — not just a contact. Verify email, map the org chart, confirm budget authority. Two-source minimum on every contact.

04

Trigger Validation

Confirm the trigger is live and recent — within 90 days. Every lead must have a confirmed reason to need your solution today, not someday.

05

Dossier Build

Full research document: company profile, decision-maker contact, pain point, recommended angle, and a personalised opening message — ready to send.

06

Multi-Channel Outreach

Cold call first (manufacturing), LinkedIn first (SaaS), email as follow-up. We manage the full sequence, replies, and follow-ups. You don't lift a finger.

07

Qualified Meeting & Handoff

Meeting booked, pre-meeting brief sent to your closer 24 hours before. We track through to conversion for our percentage fee. The loop is closed.

FAQ

Common questions,
straight answers.

How is Ertrag Go different from a regular lead gen agency?

Most agencies sell you a list. We sell you a pipeline. Every lead we deliver has a verified decision-maker, a confirmed buying trigger, full company research, and a personalised opening message. We also earn a % when deals close — so we're aligned with your revenue, not just our retainer.

You reach out as Ertrag Go — not as my company. Does that cause confusion?

Never. We position ourselves transparently as your BD partner — "I'm [Name] from Ertrag Go, working with [Your Company]." Leads respect that a specialist found them specifically. It's more credible than cold vendor emails, and it protects your brand from bad first impressions.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients see their first qualified meeting within 30 days. ICP workshop in Week 1, research and outreach in Weeks 2–3, first meetings by Week 4.

Which track should I start with?

If you're testing us for the first time, Track B (Pay Per Meeting) is zero-risk. You evaluate our lead quality with no monthly commitment. Most clients convert to Track A within 60 days once they see the results.

Do you work with both manufacturers and SaaS companies?

Yes. We run separate playbooks for each. Manufacturing clients get cold-call-first outreach. SaaS clients get LinkedIn-first. Different buyers, different methods, same quality standard.

How does the conversion percentage work?

When a lead we delivered closes into a paid contract, we earn 3–8% (Track A) or 5–12% (Track B) of the first-year deal value, capped at ₹2L per deal. Agreed in writing upfront. You report closures via our shared CRM. We operate on trust and long-term relationships.
